## How to submit

1. **Open the Google Doc template** (from the assignment link) and make a copy
2. **Copy content** from `DriveLeads_Product_Document.md` into the Google Doc template sections
3. **Take screenshots** of the 4 screens from the prototype:
   - Open `mockup-prototype/index.html` in Chrome (desktop, 1440px width)
   - Screenshot: Dashboard, Lead Listing, Lead Pipeline, Lead Details
   - Paste screenshots into the Google Doc
4. **Set Google Doc sharing** to "Anyone with the link"
5. **Submit via the Google Form** — upload the Lead Management Screen image
